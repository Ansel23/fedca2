What can we do?

reduce food waste

We can start doing our part in our everyday lives to contribute to singapore being a more sustainable country together. Small actions such as minimising food wastage in singapore is already something that will help the country and the world if we all do our part and reduce waste globally.

Reduce use of electricity

Preserving energy within our homes is impprtant for a sustainable tomorrow.switching off lights when unnecessary will help to reduce electricity consumption. This approach will not only reduces energy expenses but also reduce the limited natural resorces we have. By performing a simple and small task everyday, we can help improve the environment of our planet.
